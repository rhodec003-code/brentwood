Holiday Organization

How to Organize Before the Holidays

A calm holiday season starts before the first guest arrives. Focus on the rooms, supplies, and routines that carry the most pressure.

Holiday organizing is less about decorating and more about preparing the home to work under heavier use. Guests need space, kitchens need flow, and entertaining supplies need to be easy to find.

Start with high-impact areas

  • Edit the pantry, refrigerator overflow, and serving pieces before shopping.
  • Prepare guest rooms with linens, towels, hangers, and clear surfaces.
  • Create a gift wrap station with paper, ribbon, tags, tape, and scissors.
  • Sort decorations before they come out so broken or unused items do not return to storage.

Make storage easier after the season

The best time to improve holiday storage is while you are putting things away. Group decor by room, event, or category. Label clearly and avoid oversized mixed bins that force you to unpack everything next year.

For busy Miami families, a short organizing reset before the holidays can make entertaining, travel, and hosting feel significantly more manageable.
