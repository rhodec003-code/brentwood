Senior Move Management in Miami — What It Includes & Typical Costs

Senior move management helps older adults and families downsize, relocate, or rightsize with planning, sorting, packing, mover coordination, and full home setup.

“Senior move management” helps older adults and families downsize, relocate, or rightsize through planning, sorting, packing, coordinating movers, and fully setting up the new home. This specialty is widely recognized by NASMM, the National Association of Senior & Specialty Move Managers.

What a senior move manager can handle

  • Project planning and timelines with family updates
  • Room-by-room sorting: keep, donate, sell, digitize
  • Donation and auction coordination via licensed partners
  • Packing, labeling, and box mapping to a floor plan
  • Move-day oversight, including COIs, elevators, and dock windows
  • Unpacking and full home setup in 1–2 days
  • Safety-first placement with clear pathways and reachable shelves

Move managers plan and coordinate; transport is done by licensed movers.

Typical cost ranges and why they vary

Nationally, senior move management often bills hourly and/or by package. Published ranges cluster around $40–$80+ per hour, with total projects commonly $1,500–$5,000+ depending on home size, services, logistics, and timeline.

Cost drivers include scope and speed, building logistics, third-party fees, and family coordination.

Our Miami approach

We combine organizing expertise with move-day logistics so your loved one is settled fast — beds made, clothing hung, kitchen ready, bathrooms stocked.

Questions to ask any provider

  • What’s included?
  • Who handles COIs and reservations?
  • How many people and how long?
  • Do you coordinate movers, auction, donation, and haul-away?
  • How will remote family be kept informed?
  • Are you insured?

A simple 4-step plan

  • Plan — Walkthrough, goals, timeline, family contact.
  • Sort — Keep, donate, and sell with gentle guidance.
  • Move — Pack, label, coordinate movers and building rules.
  • Set Up — Unpack, organize, label; new home ready day one.

FAQs

Is this the same as hiring movers?

No. Movers transport; a senior move manager plans, coordinates vendors, and sets up the home.

Will insurance cover this?

Typically no. Services are private-pay, though auction proceeds or donation receipts may offset costs.

How long does a downsizing move take?

Many one- to two-bedroom moves complete in 1–3 days of on-site work.

What does it cost in Miami?

We quote after assessment. Complex urban or condo moves can be higher than national averages.
