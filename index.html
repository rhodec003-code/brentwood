About Rhode

It's not about
getting rid of things.

"It's about helping you find what's essential to you — and building a home that reflects the life you want to live."

Hello, nice to meet you! My name is Rhode, I worked in retail management for many years before transitioning to home organization. After opening retail locations for both apparel and beauty brands my entrepreneurial spirit led me to Brentwood, a project born out of a desire to blend my creative and project management skills.

Starting Brentwood Professional Organizers has been an absolute dream come true, I get to free my creative mind and build lasting impressions. My goal with Brentwood is simple, to be your trusted Home Organizer.

With an eye for detail and a deep respect for the people and spaces we serve, my team and I bring professionalism, discretion, and genuine care to every project.

Fully insured. Experienced with luxury residences, high-value collections, and estate transitions throughout Miami-Dade and Broward.
