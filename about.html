About Brentwood Organizers

The people behind a settled home.

Brentwood Organizers brings structure, discretion, and genuine care to every home we touch. Our team supports clients through luxury move-ins, home organization, and estate transitions with thoughtful planning and polished execution.

Our Approach

Calm, functional, beautifully cared for.

Hello, nice to meet you! My name is Rhode. Before launching Brentwood Organizers, I spent many years in retail management, where I developed a deep understanding for presentation, systems, and client experience. After opening retail locations for apparel and beauty brands, I found myself drawn to a more personal kind of work: helping people create homes that feel calm, functional, and beautifully cared for.

Brentwood was born from the desire to bring together my creative eye, project management background, and genuine love for helping people through life’s transitions. Whether we are preparing a home after a move, organizing everyday spaces, or supporting a larger estate transition, my goal is simple: to be a trusted partner in making your home feel settled, refined, and easier to live in.

With an eye for detail and a deep respect for the people and spaces we serve, my team and I bring professionalism, discretion, and genuine care to every project.
